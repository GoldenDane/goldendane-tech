Building Trustworthy AI for Health & Industry

GoldenDane Tech develops bespoke artificial intelligence solutions for hospitals, public health systems and engineering companies. By training models on your own anonymised data, we help you enhance patient care, improve operational efficiency and reduce risk—while you retain full control over your workflows.

Why Partner with GoldenDane?

  • Founded at the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) with access to secure, high‑performance computing
  • Compliant with the EU AI Act and GDPR—risk management, data quality, human oversight and transparency are built in
  • No off‑the‑shelf product—we co‑develop every solution with clinicians, engineers and researchers
  • Cross‑sector expertise spanning clinical diagnostics, predictive maintenance and digital twins
  • Fixed‑scope pilot projects with clear deliverables, affordable pricing and low risk

Start with a Pilot

We offer 4–6‑month pilot collaborations to develop and test machine‑learning models using anonymised or pseudonymised data—from radiology images to engineering sensor streams— hosted securely on DTU's computing cluster. Each pilot delivers a working prototype and an evaluation report, with no long‑term commitment.
