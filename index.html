Fair Play Initiative
Chess Integrity

Making the game
trustworthy again.

GoldenDane is actively engaged with the competitive chess community on one of the most consequential challenges facing the game: ensuring that online play at the highest level is genuinely fair — and seen to be. We bring the analytical capability and the institutional neutrality to approach this seriously.

◈

Custom engine development

We have developed custom modifications to state-of-the-art chess engines — enhancing evaluation frameworks to analyse positions with greater contextual precision than standard implementations allow.

⚖

Independent mediation

We work as a fully neutral third party — not representing platforms, not representing players. We are in dialogue with figures at the top of competitive chess who share our commitment to getting this right.

◇

Transparency frameworks

We design accountability standards that platforms can stand behind publicly — giving the elite player community something credible to point to, without compromising the integrity of existing systems.
