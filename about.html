About GoldenDane Tech

Who We Are

GoldenDane Tech is a Danish AI company building custom machine learning models for hospitals and public health systems. We partner directly with clinical teams to co-develop solutions using their real-world data — without needing to purchase a generic product or platform.

What Makes Us Different

  • We operate from DTU and use its secure, high-performance computing cluster
  • We do not resell — we build each model from scratch for your needs
  • We work only with anonymized or pseudonymized hospital data
  • Our fixed-scope pilot projects are affordable, fast, and transparent

GDPR and Data Privacy

All data remains within Denmark and is processed under strict GDPR controls. We act as data processor under a signed DPA, and the hospital remains the data controller. We enforce pseudonymization, access controls, and full audit logs.
