Soheil Ghoreishi

Co-Founder & CEO

Soheil Ghoreishi works at the intersection of machine learning, computational modelling, and real-world decision-making. He develops software systems that operate reliably where data is imperfect, decisions carry risk, and human expertise remains essential.

He specialises in turning complex, judgment-heavy processes, such as medical diagnostics and industrial inspection, into systems that are measurable, auditable, and deployable.

In addition to guiding technical strategy, Soheil works hands-on with model development, pilot design, and deployment planning, collaborating directly with clinicians, engineers, and technical decision-makers. He pays close attention to failure modes, edge cases, and operational constraints that determine real-world success.

He is affiliated with the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) and studies regulatory, ethical, and organisational implications of deploying AI in healthcare and industry.

Soheil is available for technical discussions, pilot planning, and early-stage collaborations.
