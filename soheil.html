Soheil Ghoreishi

Founder & CEO

Soheil Ghoreishi works at the intersection of advanced machine learning, computational modelling, and real-world decision-making. He builds software systems that operate reliably in environments where data is imperfect, decisions carry risk, and human expertise cannot be removed from the loop.

He specialises in turning complex, judgment-heavy processes—such as medical diagnostics and industrial inspection—into systems that are measurable, auditable, and deployable. His work emphasises robustness, interpretability, and practical validation over purely theoretical performance.

In addition to guiding technical strategy, Soheil works hands-on with model development, pilot design, and deployment planning, collaborating directly with clinicians, engineers, and technical decision-makers. He pays close attention to failure modes, edge cases, and operational constraints that determine real-world success.

He is affiliated with the Technical University of Denmark (DTU) and maintains an active interest in the regulatory, ethical, and organisational implications of deploying AI in healthcare and industrial contexts.

Soheil can be contacted directly regarding technical discussions, pilot scoping, and early-stage collaborations.
