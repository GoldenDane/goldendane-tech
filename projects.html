Example Project Areas

Focused AI Projects for Inspection, Review, and Decision Support

GoldenDane Tech works on applied AI projects where human experts currently spend significant time on manual review, interpretation, or judgment-heavy analysis. Our projects focus on assisting—not replacing—domain specialists by improving speed, consistency, and prioritisation.

Below are representative project areas. Each engagement starts with a narrowly scoped pilot designed to validate value with real data.

What We Can Build

  • Inspection & Review Automation – AI-assisted analysis of images, sensor data, or inspection outputs to reduce manual review time and improve consistency (e.g. pipeline inspection data, equipment scans, quality checks).
  • Risk & Anomaly Prioritisation – Models that flag high-risk cases, assets, or conditions so experts can focus attention where it matters most.
  • Decision Support for Clinical & Operational Teams – Assistive models that support—but do not automate—clinical or operational decisions using historical data and transparent logic.
  • Predictive Maintenance & Condition Monitoring – Forecasting and anomaly detection systems for industrial equipment and infrastructure.
  • Performance-Critical Evaluation Systems – Fast, custom evaluation engines for complex rule-based or simulation-driven environments.

Data Ownership, Security, and Compliance

Projects are developed using anonymised or pseudonymised data provided by the partner organisation. Systems are designed with auditability, human oversight, and data governance in mind.

Where relevant, development can be carried out on secure, GDPR-compliant infrastructure, and resulting models remain under the partner’s control.
