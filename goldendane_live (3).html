Fair Play Initiative
Chess Integrity

Making the game
trustworthy again.

GoldenDane is actively engaged with the competitive chess community on one of the most consequential challenges the game faces today: ensuring that online play at the highest level is genuinely fair and seen to be. We bring both the analytical capability and the institutional neutrality to approach this seriously.

◆

Custom engine development

We have built custom modifications to state-of-the-art chess engines, enhancing their evaluation frameworks to analyse positions with greater contextual depth than standard implementations provide.

⚖

Independent mediation

We work as a fully neutral third party, representing neither platforms nor players. We are in active dialogue with figures at the top of competitive chess who share our commitment to getting this right.

◇

Transparency frameworks

We design accountability standards that platforms can stand behind publicly, giving the elite player community something credible to point to without compromising the integrity of existing systems.
